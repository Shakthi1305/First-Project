Can i track my calories?

+

Yes !Gym Fluencer offer calorie tracking features that allow you to log your daily food intake and monitor the calories burned during workouts.

How do i log my workouts?

+

To log your workouts, follow these simple steps: Select Your Exercise: Choose the exercise or workout type from the app’s exercise library or workout plan. Input Details: Enter the key details of your session, such as: Number of sets and reps Duration and intensity Weight lifted (if applicable) Any additional notes or personal comments about the workout. Track Progress: The app will automatically track your progress, showing statistics like total calories burned, distance traveled, or weight lifted. Save Your Log: Once you’ve entered all the data, click the ‘Save’ or ‘Log Workout’ button. Your workout will be stored in your workout history for future reference. Review Your History: You can view and analyze your past workouts to track your progress over time and adjust your routine as needed. Logging workouts helps you stay organized, monitor progress, and stay on track with your fitness goals!
